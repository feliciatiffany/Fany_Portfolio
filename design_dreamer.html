DESIGN

The Greatest Dreamer — Poster Series

Project Overview

A typography exploration responding to the prompt “I am the greatest…”. I extended the statement into “I am the greatest dreamer.” The concept blurs the line between dreaming while asleep and dreaming in life, inviting multiple interpretations. I commit to keep dreaming in both senses—imagining boldly and pursuing ambitions at the scale of a fantasy world.

Design Process

  • Concept & Tone. Dual-meaning of “dreamer”; soft, ethereal mood.
  • Type Treatment. Custom title lettering; cloud-like forms.
  • Hierarchy & Readability. Clear structure across text levels.
  • Production. Composited and refined in Adobe Photoshop.
